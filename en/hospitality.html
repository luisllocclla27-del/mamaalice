Our Proposal

More than technical training, a life transformation

The Hospitality Training Center (HTC) is our flagship program. We train youth from vulnerable communities in the technical and human skills demanded by the tourism and gastronomy industry.

Our graduates not only obtain a degree, but also guaranteed internships and support until their first formal hiring.
