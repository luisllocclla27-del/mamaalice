Who We Are

A story of hope born in the Andes

Mama Alice was born from the conviction that no young person should see their future limited by their birthplace or economic status.

From Ayacucho, we build bridges between vulnerability and professionalization through comprehensive programs covering emotional health, education, and technical training.
