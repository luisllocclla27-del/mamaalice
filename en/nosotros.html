Our essence

A conviction that changed everything

Mama Alice was born from the conviction that no young person should have their future limited by their place of birth. In a context where armed conflict left deep scars, our founder decided to act.

Today we are a comprehensive ecosystem that encompasses emotional health, basic education, and technical training, with measurable results that speak for themselves.

Our values

What guides us every day

Three principles that define how we work and how we accompany each young person in their process.

Empathy

Each young person is heard, understood, and accompanied from their personal reality, without judgment.

Transparency

Every resource is audited and reported. Our donors are our partners in the mission.

Sustainability

We do not create dependency. We train for professional autonomy and full independence.

Milestones

Our journey

Over a decade building a future in Ayacucho. Scroll down to travel back in time.

2010

Foundation

Mama Alice is established as a social assistance project in the rural communities of Ayacucho, led by Frederique Kallen.

2014

Community Center

The first psychosocial support and tutoring center is inaugurated, providing refuge and education to more than 120 children and adolescents.

2018

Hospitality Center

The technical training center in gastronomy and hospitality is born, forging key alliances with the region's growing tourism sector.

2022

85% Insertion

Under local management, the highest job placement rate among technical training NGOs in the entire southern Andes is achieved.

2025

2,500 Lives

The astonishing milestone of two thousand five hundred young people trained, healed, and comprehensively supported towards a bright future is surpassed.
